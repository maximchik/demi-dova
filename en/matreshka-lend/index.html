matreshkaland

 (objects, installations, video, photo, graphics).

It’s a study of the socio-political situation in Russia. The white/blank Matreoshka (the traditional wooden doll) is a symbol of the Russian people. It is noteworthy that I shared doll by gender. Much of the activity in Russia is connected with the penis as a weapon. I test all the possible ways to interact with Matryeshkas. I put them in a state of love act or shoot at them. I force them to show their identity. I’ve got an interesting observation, that Matryeshka as native Russian doll – has a very strong and deep metaphor for life of the Russian people.
